Our Company

Gopher Delivery was founded on the simple notion that grocery delivery does not have to be expensive. What began as a small grocery delivery service on the University of Minnesota campus has grown to become one of the largest grocery delivery companies in the state of Minnesota. Today, Gopher Delivery delivers to thousands of customers all across the Twin Cities. Our customers choose from 35 different aisles with over 500 product categories and more than 10,000 items.
